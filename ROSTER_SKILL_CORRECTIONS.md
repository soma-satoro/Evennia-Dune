# Roster Characters - Skill Distribution Corrections

## Standard Character Creation Rules

1. **Primary skill** (from archetype): 6
2. **Secondary skill** (from archetype): 5  
3. **Other three skills**: 4 each
4. **Add 5 points** to any skills (max 8)
5. **Total = 28 points** (6+5+4+4+4 = 23, +5 = 28)

### Pre-Born Characters (Alia, Leto II, Ghanima)
- **Discipline 8** for all - exceptional mental powers from ancestral memories
- Different secondary skills based on personality:
  - Alia: Communicate (ruler, Voice master)
  - Leto II: Understand (Golden Path visionary)
  - Ghanima: Communicate (empathic, social)

### Combat Masters (Duncan, Stilgar, Gurney)
- **Battle 8** for all - legendary warriors
- Different secondary skills:
  - Duncan: Move (physical prowess, acrobatics)
  - Stilgar: Understand (naib wisdom)
  - Gurney: Communicate (inspiration, troubadour)

### Scholars (Irulan, Jessica)
- High Understand and Communicate
- Irulan: Understand 8 (historian, scholar focus)
- Jessica: Discipline 8 (Reverend Mother focus)
